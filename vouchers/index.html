Free Entry Vouchers

Helping community organisations give young people access to The Warehouse Indoor Skatepark

Free entry vouchers for The Warehouse Indoor Skatepark

We're launching a voucher scheme to help community organisations provide free access to The Warehouse Indoor Skatepark for those who might not otherwise be able to visit.

Each voucher contains a unique code that grants one free entry to The Warehouse during any of our Open Sessions. Recipients simply present the code at reception to gain entry.

Partner with us to distribute vouchers

We're looking for community organisations to partner with us in distributing these vouchers to those who would benefit most.

As a partner, you'll receive a batch of vouchers to distribute to your members, clients, or service users. You decide who receives them based on your knowledge of your community.

How it works:

  • Get in touch with us to discuss your organisation and how many vouchers you'd like
  • We'll provide you with printed vouchers, each with a unique code
  • You distribute the vouchers to individuals in your community
  • Recipients visit The Warehouse and present their code at reception for free entry

There's no cost to your organisation - the vouchers are provided free of charge.

Interested in partnering with us? Apply here

Who are the vouchers for?

The vouchers are designed to reach people who might not otherwise have the opportunity to visit The Warehouse, including:

  • Young people from low-income families
  • Those facing barriers to accessing leisure activities
  • People who haven't tried skateboarding, scootering, or roller skating before
  • Anyone who would benefit from a safe, welcoming space to be active

Benefits of skateparks

Skateparks provide significant physical and mental health benefits, particularly for young people:

Physical activity

Skateboarding, scootering, and roller skating are excellent forms of exercise that don't feel like traditional sports.

Mental wellbeing

90.7% of our users say The Warehouse improves their mental health.

Community & belonging

A welcoming space where young people can meet others and feel part of a supportive community.
