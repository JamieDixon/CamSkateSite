Types of sessions

Open sessions
🛹 Skateboards🛼 Rollerskates/blades

Open sessions are our most frequent session at The Warehouse - open to all skateboarders/surfskaters/longboarders, rollerskaters and rollerbladers.

Beginner sessions
🛹 Skateboards🛼 Rollerskates/blades

New to skating? Beginner sessions are a supportive, quieter session that are designed to give beginners the space they need to build confidence and skills.

Junior Jam - Under 10s
🛴 Scooters🛹 Skateboards🛼 Rollerskates/blades

Our Saturday Junior Jam is a fun, friendly session where kids (and their parents) can learn, practise and play together in a safe, relaxed space.

Scooter session - Over 8s
🛴 Scooters

Our weekly Scooter Session is open to all scooter riders aged 8 years and older.

Monthly sessions

Girl Skate Night
💖 Free!🛹 Skateboards🛼 Rollerskates/blades

Girl Skate Night is a monthly skate night run for and by women, girls, and non-binary people. It's free entry and has a nice welcoming community.

Queer Skate Night
💖 Free!🛹 Skateboards🛼 Rollerskates/blades

Queer Skate Night is a monthly skate night run for and by the LGBTQ+ community.

Quads & Blades
🛼 Rollerskates/blades

Quads & Blades night is a monthly night run for and by rollerskaters and rollerbladers.

Pipe & Slippers - Over 30s
🛹 Skateboards🛼 Rollerskates/blades

The monthly 'Pipe and Slippers' night is our dedicated session for skaters aged 30 years and older. There's a beginner session at the start for those looking to build more confidence!

Prices & Memberships

Regular sessions (open, beginner, junior, scooter, quads & blades, over 30s) cost just £5! Pay when you arrive and skate until the end of the session. No slots or wristbands or pre-booking. Just pay and skate.

Monthly memberships cost just £30 for unlimited entry to regular sessions for 28 days. You can purchase a monthly membership at reception in the park.
