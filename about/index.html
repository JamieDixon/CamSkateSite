About Cam Skate

Cam Skate is the voice of skaters in Cambridge.

We believe that skating is a positive force and should be accessible to as many people as possible. To this end, we campaign for better skating facilities in the city, involve skaters in the planning process and local elections, and organise events and provide coaching to get more people into the sport.
